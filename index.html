About Us

At FROiD Cooling Products, we create innovative storage solutions designed for individuals, healthcare providers, and organizations who need to keep medications safe—whether during travel or in extreme climates.

Our flagship product, the patent-protected FROiD Storage Wallet, is engineered to deliver a portable, reusable, and reliable way to protect life-saving medications anywhere in the world.

Our mission is to lead the world in portable storage solutions for critical medications. With rapid growth across India and abroad, we are proud to partner with healthcare providers, NGOs, and global brands to make safe medication storage accessible worldwide.
